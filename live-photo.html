JKSSB Live Photo Capture – Step by Step Guide

When prompted, allow camera access in your browser.

If permission is denied, live photo will not work.

Use Google Chrome for best results.

If multiple cameras are available, select the correct one.

Rear camera is recommended for clearer image.

Front camera may show mirror preview.

Sometimes the preview appears mirrored.

Do not worry if face looks reversed in preview.

Keep your face straight and centered.

Sit in a well-lit area with light facing your face.

Use a plain, light-colored background.

Avoid shadows on face.

This error occurs due to hand movement or low light.

Place phone on a stable surface if possible.

Wait 2–3 seconds before capture.

Uploaded photo and live photo must match.

Avoid spectacles, caps, masks during capture.

Use a recent uploaded photograph.

Camera not opening: refresh page and re-allow permission.

Black screen: close other apps using camera.

Error on mobile: try laptop or desktop if available.

Do not use filters or beauty mode.

Keep neutral expression.

Follow on-screen instructions carefully.
