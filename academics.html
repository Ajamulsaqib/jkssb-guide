How to Fill Academic Details in JKSSB

Clear explanation to avoid mistakes and rejection

While filling the application on the official portal of :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}, candidates must carefully enter their academic qualifications. Wrong entry can lead to rejection during document verification.

1️⃣ Matriculation (10th)

  • Enter details exactly as mentioned on your 10th marksheet
  • Board name, roll number, passing year must be correct
  • Upload clear and readable marksheet

2️⃣ Higher Secondary (12th)

  • Fill only if required for the post
  • Stream should match the eligibility criteria
  • Marks should be entered correctly (percentage or CGPA as mentioned)

3️⃣ Graduation (General)

This section is for candidates who have completed a general degree like BA, BSc, BCom, etc.

  • Enter university name correctly
  • Fill passing year and roll number
  • Upload final year marksheet or degree certificate

4️⃣ Graduation (Technical / Professional)

This section is only for technical or professional degrees such as:

  • B.Tech / B.E
  • BCA / MCA
  • Diploma (if required by the post)
⚠️ Do not fill this section if your post does not require a technical or professional qualification.

❓ Should I Fill Both Graduation Sections?

  • ✔ Fill only one section if the post demands a specific qualification
  • ✔ Fill both only if the advertisement clearly mentions both
  • ❌ Do not guess or overfill details

Common Academic Mistakes

  • Wrong passing year
  • Name mismatch with certificates
  • Uploading wrong marksheet
  • Filling technical section unnecessarily
✅ Always cross-check details with the official advertisement PDF.
