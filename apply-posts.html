Apply for JKSSB Posts – Step by Step Guide

Login using your registered username and password.

Make sure your account is activated before login.

If you forget password, use the "Forgot Password" option.

After login, go to the "Active Advertisements" section.

Read the notification carefully before clicking Apply.

Check eligibility: qualification, age, domicile, and category.

Click the Apply button against the post you want to apply for.

If Apply button is disabled, you may not be eligible.

Some posts may require application fee.

Personal details will be auto-filled from your account.

Carefully check academic, category, and domicile details.

Do not edit details unless allowed by the portal.

Upload required documents in PDF format only.

File size should be within prescribed limits.

Upload documents only in the correct fields.

Pay application fee through online mode only.

Fee once paid is non-refundable.

Save payment receipt for future reference.

Preview the complete application carefully.

Check name, post applied for, and uploaded documents.

Errors after final submission may not be corrected.

Click Final Submit after checking all details.

Download and print the application form.

Keep printout safely for future reference.
