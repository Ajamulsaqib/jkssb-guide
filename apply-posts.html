How to Apply for JKSSB Posts

Complete guide to apply correctly for various posts

Step 1: Login to JKSSB Portal

Login using your registered credentials on the official website of :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

Step 2: Check Active Advertisements

  • Go to the Advertisements / Notifications section
  • Open the latest advertisement PDF
  • Check eligibility, qualification & cadre carefully

Step 3: Select the Post

Click on the Apply button for the post you are eligible for.

Understanding Cadres (VERY IMPORTANT)

  • District Cadre: Only candidates from that district are eligible
  • Division Cadre: Candidates from the entire division (Jammu/Kashmir)
  • UT Cadre: Candidates from all over J&K UT

Step 4: Fill Application Sections

  • Personal details
  • Academic qualifications
  • Document upload
  • Post preference

Step 5: Preview Before Final Submission

Carefully check all details before clicking Final Submit. Once submitted, changes are usually not allowed.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Applying for posts without eligibility
  • Ignoring cadre requirements
  • Wrong academic section filled
  • Not previewing the application
  • Last-day submission rush
