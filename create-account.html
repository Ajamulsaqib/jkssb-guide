How to Create JKSSB Account

Step-by-step guide for new candidates

Step 1: Visit Official Website

Go to the official website of :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0} and click on New Registration.

Step 2: Fill Basic Details

  • Full Name (as per 10th marksheet)
  • Date of Birth
  • Mobile Number
  • Email ID

⚠️ Do not use nickname or short name.

Step 3: Verify Mobile & Email

An OTP will be sent to your registered mobile number and email ID. Enter OTP carefully.

Step 4: Set Password

  • Password should contain letters, numbers & symbols
  • Do not share your password with anyone

Step 5: Login Successfully

After successful registration, login using your credentials.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Creating multiple accounts
  • Wrong date of birth
  • Using another person’s mobile number
  • Mismatch in name and certificates
