Create JKSSB Account – Step by Step Guide

Enter your name exactly as written in your Matric (10th) certificate.

Do not use titles like Shri, Mr, Ms, Dr, or extra spaces.

Any mismatch in name can cause rejection during document verification.

Enter your father’s name exactly as mentioned in your Matric certificate.

Do not add words like Late, Shri, or initials.

Select your Date of Birth carefully.

Date of Birth must match your Matric certificate or School Leaving Certificate.

Date of Birth cannot be changed later.

Select your gender as mentioned in official records.

Choose marital status correctly. Wrong selection may affect eligibility for some posts.

Enter a valid and active email ID.

Account activation link and password recovery details are sent to this email.

Check spam folder if activation mail is not received.

Enter your 10-digit mobile number.

Do not add +91 or 0 before the number.

OTP and important updates are sent to this number.

Select your category carefully (OM, SC, ST, OBC, RBA, etc.).

If you select domicile as Yes, you must upload a valid domicile certificate during application.

Wrong category selection may lead to rejection.

Create a strong password as per instructions shown on the screen.

Password should be remembered safely for future logins.

Enter captcha exactly as displayed.

If captcha is unclear, refresh it before entering.

Recheck all entered details before clicking Submit.

After submission, check your email for account activation link.

Without activating the account, you cannot apply for any post.
